Sinquerim is located on the north side of Baga beach and hasbeautiful, wide stretches of endless sand. Sinquerim Beach happens to be the most famous tourist beach in North Goa. Here, one can enjoy world class adventure sport activities like parasailing and scuba diving. And not to forget, it is here that the now famous Sunburn festival is held each year during the month of December.

facts
The beach at Sinquerim lies adjacent to the Aguada beach. Near the beach one can see the famous historical fort Aguada that was built by the Portuguese to defend them from any attack from the sea route.
